The Next

s

The Next is a cancer based support program for patients, spouses, parents, siblings and adult children.  We understand that cancer affects everyone and not just the person with the diagnosis.  This is NOT a support group, but a support person.  You are not the first cancer fighter, and sadly will not be the last.  The goal of The Next is to help The Next one to walk your same path. 

When you sign up for this program, you will be paired with a person in our program that has walked as similar a path as we can find.  We are considering age, cancer type, stage of treatment, etc.  You will become their Next and they will fill a “sponsor” roll.  They can share their personal experiences and field more personal questions that perhaps a doctor can’t answer for you.  Example: Did you experience mucositis with chemo?  Did you bring your children with you to transplant?  How did you tell your family your diagnosis was terminal?  How did you feel when your wife lost her hair?

Eventually our hope is that we can pair you with your Next.   We believe you do not need to be past your cancer journey to support The Next one behind you.  It can be healing to help others and share information you learned in your journey.  This is an opportunity to not walk this path alone, but to have a hand to hold as you walk it together!
